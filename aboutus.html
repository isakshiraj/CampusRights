WHO ARE WE?



We are a group of university students passionate about promoting awareness of student rights and ensuring justice on campus. As students ourselves, we understand the importance of knowing your rights and having the confidence to stand up for them. This platform was created to provide easy access to information about students' rights, university policies, and available support, empowering students to make informed decisions and prevent injustices. Our mission is to foster a campus environment where every student feels safe, respected, and supported. By raising awareness and providing resources, we aim to create a community where fairness and equality are prioritized, and no student has to face injustice alone.
