Anti Ragging

RAGGING IS A CRIMINAL OFFENSE AND THE CULPRITS WILL ATTRACT PUNITIVE ACTION

Ragging, defined as any form of physical or psychological abuse inflicted upon students within educational institutions, is strictly prohibited under both legal statutes and institutional policies. It encompasses behaviors such as bullying, harassment, intimidation, and humiliation, all of which pose serious threats to students' physical and mental well-being. To combat this, Anti Ragging Committees is tasked with monitoring and preventing incidents. Students are urged to promptly report any instances of ragging to these committees or other designated authorities to ensure swift intervention and support for victims, thereby fostering a safe and respectful campus environment conducive to learning.
