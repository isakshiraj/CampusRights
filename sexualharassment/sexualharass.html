Sexual Harassment



What constitutes Sexual Harassment?

Sexual Harassment includes unwelcome Sexual Behaviour of direct or implied nature such as -

  • Physical contact and advances
  • Request for sexual favors
  • Sexually colored remarks
  • Showing pornography
  • Unwelcome physical, verbal or non-verbal sexual conduct

How does one prepare a complaint?

Write exactly what happened with details, dates, names of

  • Witnesses & document, if any
  • Be specific about the incident/incidents.
  • Be honest, as false complaints can invite penalties.
  • You need to file a complaint of sexual harassment within three months of the incident

Where to report

  1. Submit the complaint: Directly at the office of the ICC, Room No. 116, (Ground Floor, Administration Department, IGDTUW, Delhi 110006)
  2. Send an email to: iccoffice@igdtuw.ac.in
  3. Can contact women Helpline Number - 181
  4. Contact Emergency Response Support System Number - 112
  5. If you are in college, then contact the College ICC
